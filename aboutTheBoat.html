Overview

Our entry this year is a 1.3 meter long custom built fiberglass boat. It uses a combination of an electronic compass, a GPS receiver and a wind sensor to detect its orientation, position and the wind direction. These are connected to a Raspberry Pi, which is a cheap single board ARM based computer, running Linux, and an Arduino Uno micro-controller board. The sail is controlled by an electronic winch and the rudder by a servo motor, both controlled by the Arduino.
